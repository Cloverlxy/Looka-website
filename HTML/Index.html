Why Looka?

The word 'Looka' was actually inspired by the book Lykke. How do we define happiness in our life? What are your feeling towards your life? Finding happiness is crucial to our lives. When we are working, studying or playing, everything that we did, we try to find happiness in it. This book explain happiness and ways that we can pursuit it. If you are familiar with the word 'Hygge' it means a mood of coziness which can basically refers to anything like listening to your favourite music before sleeping, reading a book by the window with delightful scenary or even lighting up a candle and have a cup of tea. That is why, we want to bring happiness to you! In this cottage inspired tearoom, where handmade desserts were served with teas, good books and music, happiness can be shared!
